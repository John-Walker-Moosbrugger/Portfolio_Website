About Me

I’ve been designing, building and breaking things for as long as I can remember; but my first real exposure to design as a skill was freshman year of college. I dove straight in. First with theatre, designing scenery and then branching out until I found myself working on everything from lunar robots and hyperloop systems, to augmented reality CAD programs and websites.

I have a particular passion for design in service of a story. Whether it’s the story of a startup or a vision for the future of transportation, finding the core of an idea and telling that story.

In service of that goal I have found myself becoming a web-developer, animator, video editor, VR artist graphic designer and much more. The more work I do the more the medium becomes just another tool and I have put my whole focus into the design.
